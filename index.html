Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Quran Mode does not collect or share personal data. All app data, including reading history, streak, and settings are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers.

Subscriptions

We use RevenueCat to process subscription payments. RevenueCat may collect anonymous usage data. Their privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Prayer Times

Prayer times are fetched from the AlAdhan API (aladhan.com) using your entered city and country. No location data is stored or shared with us.

Notifications

Notification preferences are stored locally on your device only.
